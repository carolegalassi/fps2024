Frequently Asked Questions About Homegoing Services

Homegoing services are a cherished tradition in many communities, offering a unique and uplifting way to celebrate the life and faith of a loved one. Whether you are planning a service or attending as a guest, you may have questions about what to expect, how to prepare, and how to honor the occasion respectfully. Below are the top 10 questions-homegoing-service topics people ask, with clear answers to help guide you through the process.

Question Summary Answer Resource
What is a homegoing service? A faith-based funeral tradition, especially in African American communities, focused on celebrating the soul's return to heaven. Shopify Article
How is a homegoing different from a traditional funeral? Homegoings are typically more celebratory, featuring gospel music, testimonies, and joyful remembrance. Google Doc
What should I wear to a homegoing service? Dress respectfully; traditional black attire is common, but some families request specific colors to honor the loved one. YouTube Channel
