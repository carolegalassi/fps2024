What Are Funeral Booklets?

Funeral booklets are printed programs distributed at memorial services, funerals, or celebrations of life. They serve as both a guide for the ceremony and a lasting tribute to the deceased. Typically, these booklets include the order of service, personal tributes, photos, poems, and other meaningful content. Creating a thoughtful funeral booklet helps guests remember the person and provides comfort during a difficult time.

Key Elements to Include

Each funeral booklet is unique, but most include several core elements. Here is a quick overview of what you might incorporate:

Section Purpose Tips
Cover Page Features name, dates, and a photo Choose a high-resolution image
Order of Service Outlines the ceremony List speakers, readings, and songs
Personal Tributes Shares memories and messages Invite family and friends to contribute

How to Personalize a Funeral Booklet

Personalization is key to making a funeral booklet meaningful. Consider adding favorite poems, scripture, or song lyrics that were important to your loved one. Use their favorite colors or a theme that reflects their personality. Many families include a collage of photographs or a timeline of significant life events. The more personal touches you add, the more the booklet will resonate with guests and serve as a treasured keepsake.

Design and Printing Options

Funeral booklets can be designed using templates or created from scratch. There are many online resources and services available to help with layout, printing, and delivery. You can print them at home, at a local print shop, or order professionally printed booklets online.
