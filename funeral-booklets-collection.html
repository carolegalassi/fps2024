Funeral Booklets by The Funeral Program Site

Funeral booklets by The Funeral Program Site are designed to offer both elegance and flexibility for honoring a loved one’s memory. Available in two practical sizes—legal (8.5" x 14") and tabloid (11" x 17")—these templates begin as an 8-sided booklet and can be expanded with unlimited pages as needed. Each booklet provides ample room for an obituary, order of service, poems, scriptures, photo collages, and special tributes.

Families can personalize every detail to reflect the unique life being celebrated. Whether you choose to print at home or through a professional printer, these funeral booklets create a timeless keepsake for friends and family to treasure.
