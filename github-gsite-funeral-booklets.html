What Is a Funeral Booklet?

A funeral booklet is a printed memorial that typically includes the order of service, photos, poems, scriptures, and personal tributes. It serves as both a program for the ceremony and a cherished keepsake for attendees. On platform 9, families often choose to personalize these booklets with favorite memories, meaningful quotes, and unique design elements that capture the spirit of their loved one.

Designing a Meaningful Booklet

When designing funeral booklets, consider the tone and style that best reflects the individual being remembered. Select a color palette, fonts, and imagery that feel appropriate and comforting. Many choose to include a cover photo, a timeline of the person’s life, and heartfelt messages from family and friends. Templates are available to simplify the process, but adding personal touches such as handwritten notes or custom artwork can make your booklet truly special.

Essential Elements to Include

  • Cover with name, dates, and a photo
  • Order of service or ceremony outline
  • Obituary or life story
  • Favorite poems, scriptures, or quotes
  • Photo collage or gallery
  • Acknowledgments and thank you notes
  • Details for reception or repast, if applicable
