What Is Funeral Paper?

Funeral paper refers to the specialty paper used for printing memorial programs, prayer cards, bookmarks, and other keepsakes distributed during a funeral or memorial service. The right paper enhances the look and feel of these items, providing a sense of quality and respect. It is important to select paper that is both durable and elegant, as these keepsakes are often cherished for years to come.

Types of Funeral Paper

There are several types of paper commonly used for funeral programs and related materials. The most popular options include:

  • Matte Paper: Offers a smooth, non-glossy finish that is easy to write on and provides a classic look.
  • Glossy Paper: Delivers a shiny, vibrant finish that makes colors and photos stand out, ideal for photo-heavy designs.
  • Parchment Paper: Features a subtle texture and a timeless, elegant appearance, often used for traditional or vintage themes.
  • Cardstock: Thicker and more rigid than standard paper, perfect for bookmarks, prayer cards, and covers.

How to Choose the Best Funeral Paper

When selecting funeral paper, consider the following factors:

  • Theme and Tone: Match the paper style to the service's atmosphere. For a formal service, parchment or matte may be ideal. For a celebration of life, glossy paper can add vibrancy.
  • Printing Method: Ensure the paper is compatible with your printer, especially if you are printing at home. Some specialty papers work best with laser printers, while others are suitable for inkjet.
  • Durability: Choose a heavier weight for items meant to last, like bookmarks or prayer cards.
  • Budget: Higher-quality paper may cost more, but it adds a significant touch of elegance and professionalism.
