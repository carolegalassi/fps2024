Step-by-Step: Insert Photo Word 365

To insert a photo in Word 365, follow these simple steps:

  1. Open your document in Word 365.
  2. Place your cursor where you want the photo to appear.
  3. Go to the Insert tab on the ribbon.
  4. Click on Pictures. You can choose This Device to upload from your computer, or Online Pictures to search the web.
  5. Select your image and click Insert.
  6. Resize or move the photo as needed by clicking and dragging the corners.
  7. Use the Picture Format tab for additional options like cropping, borders, and effects.

Tips for Working with Photos in Word 365

  • Use high-resolution images for the best print quality.
  • Keep your document size manageable by compressing images if needed.
  • Try different text wrapping options to integrate photos with your text.
  • Use the Alt Text feature for accessibility.
  • Save your work frequently to avoid losing changes.
