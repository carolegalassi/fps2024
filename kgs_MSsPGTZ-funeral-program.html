Honoring a loved one starts with choosing the right funeral program to help guide guests through the memorial service. These templates offer an organized way to share the order of events, photographs, and heartfelt memories, making it easier for families to focus on the emotional aspects of the day. With editable formats and digital convenience, they bring professionalism and personalization together in one simple solution.


Today’s funeral program designs are available in a variety of layouts including bifold, trifold, gatefold, and digital slideshow options. Whether your style leans toward traditional, spiritual, floral, or minimal design, these templates can be easily customized with software such as Google Slides or Microsoft Word. This makes it easy to reflect the unique spirit of your loved one within the program’s design.

Using digital funeral program also makes it simple to distribute to friends and family who are unable to attend the service in person.

Templates typically include space for an obituary, order of service, favorite verses or poems, and family acknowledgments. This format ensures that everything meaningful is presented in a cohesive and respectful way.


Many families find comfort in selecting funeral program that are ready to download and edit instantly. These professionally designed layouts are not only time-saving but also aesthetically thoughtful. With a wide range of styles available, it’s easy to tailor the look and tone of the program to match the type of tribute being planned.


For a live example, you can preview one of our sample funeral program to see how elegant and practical these designs can be. To understand your consumer rights while planning a funeral, we recommend reviewing the FTC’s official Funeral Rule. It provides transparency on pricing and ensures fair treatment during a sensitive time.
