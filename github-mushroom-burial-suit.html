What Is a Mushroom Burial Suit?

The mushroom burial suit, sometimes called an infinity burial suit, is a biodegradable garment infused with mushroom spores and other microorganisms. When worn by the deceased during burial, the suit activates a natural decomposition process. The mushrooms break down the body, neutralize toxins, and enrich the surrounding soil, helping to restore the ecosystem and reduce the carbon footprint of traditional burial methods.

This approach is gaining popularity among those who wish to leave a positive environmental legacy. The suit is made from organic materials and is free from chemicals, metals, and plastics commonly found in conventional burial attire.

Benefits of Choosing a Mushroom Burial Suit

  • Reduces environmental impact by avoiding embalming fluids and non-biodegradable caskets
  • Promotes rapid and clean decomposition through natural fungal processes
  • Restores soil health and supports new plant growth
  • Provides a meaningful, symbolic return to nature
  • Aligns with the values of those seeking green burial options
